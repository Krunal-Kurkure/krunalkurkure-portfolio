Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

At CodeBy KK, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our website or engaging in our freelance development services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so—for example, to provide our services, to communicate with you, or to make our website better. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, mobile number, and project details provided via our contact forms.
  • Payment Information: Billing details securely processed via third-party providers (like Stripe or PayPal). We do not store your credit card data on our servers.
  • Project Assets: Any code, API keys, logins, or intellectual property you share with us strictly for the purpose of completing your project.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with you regarding project updates, quotes, and revisions.
  • Deliver the requested software, applications, or design services.
  • Process payments and issue invoices.
  • Maintain a private portfolio of completed work (only with your explicit consent).

3. Data Security & Confidentiality

All client data, API secrets, and proprietary code are handled with the utmost security. We implement standard encryption practices and restrict access to project files. Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) can be signed upon request before any sensitive data is exchanged.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like GitHub for repository hosting, Vercel/AWS for deployment, and Stripe for payments). These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

1. Scope of Work

CodeBy KK provides full-stack web and mobile development, UI/UX design, and AI integration services. The specific details, deliverables, and timelines for each project will be outlined in a separate, agreed-upon Statement of Work (SOW) or digital invoice before development begins.

2. Payments & Milestones

Due to the custom nature of software development, payments are generally structured in milestones:

  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure a spot in the development queue and initiate the design/planning phase.
  • The remaining 50% balance is due strictly upon project completion, approval, and prior to the final transfer of source code or production deployment.
  • Payments for pre-built source code from the "Projects Store" are due 100% upfront.

3. Revisions & Alterations

Custom development packages include a specific number of revision rounds during the UI/UX phase. Once the development phase has begun, major changes to the project scope (feature creep) will be billed at an hourly rate or re-quoted entirely.

CodeBy KK retains the right to display non-sensitive screenshots of the completed project in our portfolio, unless a strict NDA prohibits it.

Custom packages include a standard period of bug-fixing support (typically 1 to 3 months) following deployment. This covers errors in the original code. It does not cover new features, server hosting fees, or breakages caused by third-party API changes.
